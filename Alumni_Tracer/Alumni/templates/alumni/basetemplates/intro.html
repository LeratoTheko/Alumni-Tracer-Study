Alumni Tracer Study

The Alumni Tracer Study is an essential tool for understanding the impact of our education on graduates' careers. It helps the university track the progress of alumni in their professional lives, gather feedback, and improve educational programs.

Benefits to the University

    Improves curriculum based on real-world feedback.
    Enhances alumni engagement and community building.
    Strengthens university reputation through successful alumni.

Benefits to Alumni

    Opens doors to networking opportunities.
    Facilitates access to university resources and support.
    Allows alumni to contribute to the growth of future graduates.

Benefits to Current Students

    Provides insights into career paths and industry expectations.
    Offers mentorship opportunities from successful alumni.
    Enables participation in career development programs.
    Connects students with internship and job opportunities.

Career Services

Our Career Services provide alumni and students with valuable resources, including job search assistance, resume building, interview preparation, and career counseling. We aim to empower our community to achieve professional success.
